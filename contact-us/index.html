Why use EcoTech?

Our unique cleaning system is environmentally friendly, 100% caustic- and solvent-free. This means you benefit from a safe working environment and a fume-free home. Furthermore, this means ovens and appliances are completely ready to use immediately after cleaning!

 We check and test all carpets and fabrics for colour fastness and shrinkage before cleaning and look after your property as we would for our own.

 Our technicians are professional, fully trained and insured providing a top quality friendly and efficient service in just a couple of hours.

 Our customers are often overwhelmed by the quality of the service that is provided by our EcoTech Oven & Carpet Cleaning technicians.

 Our goal is to complete every job to the very highest standard.

What products are used?

Our oven cleaning service technicians clean ovens to near showroom condition within a maximum of 2 hours using a unique eco-friendly and caustic-free range of EcoTech Oven Cleaning solutions.

Non-toxic oven cleaning products are seldom found on supermarket shelves; so achieving this level of cleaning on your own is almost impossible. Our service is second to none, leaving pristine appliances with no mess and no fuss.

Why can't I do it myself?

The results we can achieve with our professional equipment and eco-friendly products can’t be beaten by the products you can buy in stores.

Our expertise stretches far beyond just cleaning ovens. We also clean: gas hobs, electric hobs and ceramic hobs, extractor fans, filters and hoods, microwave and combi-microwave ovens, barbeques and grills. We provide a vast selection of traditional stove cleaning services, including the cleaning of ‘Range style’ ovens, cookers, stoves and Agas.

We do more than just clean carpets, rugs and upholstery – we can remove most stains. We can also protect your carpets, rugs and upholstery after cleaning prolonging their life and preventing future soiling.

My oven is self cleaning!

Yes, our technicians are fully trained to clean self-cleaning ovens, and the range of our eco friendly products are specifically designed to cope with all oven types.

Self-cleaning ovens comprise of self-clean interiors and/or liners, designed to burn off debris left behind from cooking. However, many do not reach the high temperature needed to produce the required result. Many of our customers express how difficult it can be to attempt cleaning such ovens themselves, as these liners ‘hide’ the majority of grease and fat build-up. Our technician will be able to remove all fat, grease and carbon deposits that could otherwise have posed a fire risk especially around the main heating element.

What training do the technicians have?

You can be assured that EcoTech Oven & Carpet Cleaning offer the highest standard of cleaning. Your home and appliances are in safe hands, as all our technicians have been trained during a two-week intensive and practical course. They also attend regular seminars and courses for the latest techniques and products.

Do the technicians carry spares?

Yes. All EcoTech Oven Cleaning technicians carry a stock of replacement lamps and a range of extractor filters.

How often should I clean my oven?

How often our customers need their appliances cleaned depends on what you cook and how often you use them. Regular use of the oven for traditionally prepared meals can require a quarterly or bi-annual deep clean, to avoid the build up of fat, grease and carbon deposits that could pose a potential fire hazard. For customers who rarely use their oven, or use it primarily for ready meals, we would recommend an annual clean to revive the working components of your oven. Our oven cleaning service will help prolong the life of your cooking appliance.

How often should I clean my carpets?

We recommend that domestic carpets should be cleaned every 12 months. If you have allergies or suffer from asthma then doctors suggest this should be every 6 – 8 months. Using a standard vacuum does not remove dust mites or bacteria – these will only be destroyed using a professional extraction system. Our carpet cleaning service will help prolong the life span of your carpets and upholstery.

How long do carpets take to dry?

Most carpets (depending on the fibre) take approximatey 2 – 3 hours to dry. We use blowers to help dry high traffic areas.

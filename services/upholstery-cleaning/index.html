Fabric Upholstery

The only way to obtain effective upholstery cleaning results (much like cleaning your clothes) is to emulsify soiling (breaking down stains) to liquid and then removing by hot water extraction (rinsing).  For this reason we use the most advanced cleaning products and latest equipment using the hot water extraction method.

Leather Upholstery

The cleaning of leather suites is carried out by hand using specialised gentle leather cleaning solutions.  After cleaning we treat the leather with a treatment of leather conditioner. By moisturising the leather it brings back its suppleness and colour.  As a result your furniture will look and feel revitalised.

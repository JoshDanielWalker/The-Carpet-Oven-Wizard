The Carpet & Oven Wizard Cleaning has vast experience and with our professionally trained technicians we are the perfect people when you need to have your cooking appliance looking like it just left the shop. Therefore you will be happy for your visitors to take a look at what is being prepared in your oven. We are very aware of the environment and all our products are eco-friendly, biodegradable, non-toxic and non-caustic.

Your cooker is a major feature in your kitchen and something that we all rely on. Having your oven cleaned professionally not only will it look fantastic but it will also be more effective and last longer. Also, your food will taste better as all the burnt on carbons can affect the taste and smell of your food. Once your grill is cleaned your smoke alarm is a lot less likely to be set off.

Our oven cleaning service is available for all makes and models of ovens. This can be a single oven, a range or Aga. Also hobs, extractor hoods, microwaves and bbq’s. We are fully insured and our team are friendly, reliable and fully trained.  So you can can feel safe when using our services.

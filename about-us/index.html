About Us

About Us

In this section you can read about us and our team. We are a small family run business specialising in oven, carpet and upholstery cleaning. Also, we are not a franchise business. EcoTech oven cleaning service and EcoTech carpet cleaning service produces perfect results every time, no matter how dirty your oven or carpets and upholstery are.

Our reliable, high quality service means that we’re always the right choice for your oven, carpet and upholstery cleaning needs.

Our Values

Eco Friendly Procedures

We only use eco-friendly, fully biodegradable, non-toxic and non-corrosive products which are therefore safe for all your family and pets.

Top Of The Range Equipment

We provide expert cleaning of carpets and rugs using the latest technology, equipment and products which promises high quality results.

Qualified Trained & Insured Technicians

All our team are fully trained, qualified and of course insured for your peace of mind. 

Our People

More about us, When EcoTech cleaning services started in 2011 it consisted of George & Linda.  EcoTech has slowly grown to a team of 6.  The team are all committed to provide a professional, reliable, polite and high quality oven cleaning, carpet cleaning and upholstery cleaning service to all our customers.

This is shown in our high percentage of repeat clients and their recommendations to their friends, family and colleagues.

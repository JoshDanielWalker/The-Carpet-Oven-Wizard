About Us

Specialist oven and carpet cleaning business providing quality service and value for money. Our mission, to provide our customers with a ‘one stop shop’ of professional specialist cleaning services.

Most of our business is for previously delighted customers and their friends, family and colleagues. Also, because we provide several different cleaning services in your home, you only need to deal with one company.

Our philosophy has always been simple – we aim to carry out the most effective clean possible to your oven, carpets and upholstery, whilst offering genuine value for money. To do this, we use only the best, most advanced equipment and fully biodegradable cleaning solutions available today. This, together with our vast experience, ensures you can be confident that we know what we are doing and will respect both you and your home as we carry out your oven and carpet cleaning.
